About Concrete Calculator Yards

Our concrete calculator yards tool helps you determine the exact amount of concrete needed for your project in cubic yards. This is the standard unit used by ready-mix concrete suppliers in the United States.

Why Calculate in Cubic Yards?

  • Ready-mix concrete is typically ordered in cubic yards
  • Concrete trucks usually carry 8-10 cubic yards per load
  • Pricing is often quoted per cubic yard
  • Standard unit for construction industry in the US

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Select the shape of your concrete structure
  2. Enter the dimensions in feet and inches
  3. Get instant results in cubic yards
  4. Add 10% waste factor for ordering

Common Applications

  • Slabs: Driveways, patios, garage floors
  • Footings: Foundation footings, deck posts
  • Columns: Support columns, sonotube footings
  • Walls: Retaining walls, basement walls

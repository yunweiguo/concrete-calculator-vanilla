About Quikrete Bags

Quikrete is a leading brand of pre-mixed concrete. Their bags come in standard sizes (40lb, 60lb, 80lb), each yielding a specific volume of mixed concrete. This calculator helps you estimate exactly how many bags you need for your project.

  • 40 lb bag ≈ 0.30 cubic feet
  • 60 lb bag ≈ 0.45 cubic feet
  • 80 lb bag ≈ 0.60 cubic feet

Always buy a little extra to account for spillage and variations.
